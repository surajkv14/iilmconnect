
# Smart Mess – AI-Powered Campus Dining

Smart Mess is a comprehensive digital transformation platform for university dining halls. It optimizes meal planning, reduces food waste, and provides a seamless booking experience for students and staff.

## Features

- **Advanced Booking**: Students reserve meals 24h in advance to optimize kitchen prep.
- **Operational Portal**: Mess staff manage complex menus (Veg/Non-Veg/Vegan) and live serving stats.
- **Sustainability Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of waste saved and consumption trends.
- **Direct Feedback**: Students voice opinions through "Mess Pulse" polls and direct messaging.
- **Admin Control**: Complete oversight of roles and campus-wide dining records.

## Tech Stack

- **Next.js 15 (App Router)**
- **Firebase (Auth & Firestore)**
- **ShadCN UI & Tailwind CSS**
- **Recharts for Dining Analytics**
